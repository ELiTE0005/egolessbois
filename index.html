Our Story

It all started in 2015 when seven strangers met at school. What began as a casual conversation over shared interests quickly evolved into a deep friendship that has stood the test of time.

The name Egoless Bois came from our core philosophy - putting friendship above personal egos. We've supported each other through thick and thin, celebrating successes and overcoming challenges together.

From road trips across the country to late-night gaming sessions, our adventures have shaped us into a tight-knit group that values loyalty, fun, and authenticity above all else.
